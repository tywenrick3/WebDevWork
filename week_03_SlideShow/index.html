"THE GREY AREA BETWEEN BLACK AND WHITE"

Born in Illinois, Abloh graduated from the University of Wisconsin Madison, with a passion for architecture. Stationed in Rome, Italy in 2009 Abloh met and collaborated with Kanye West interning in the same class for Fendi. Abloh's distinct design and creative choices quickly attracted attention from higher ups most prominently Louis Vouittons CEO Michal Burke. Abloh went on to found multiple companies, his second being his crown jewel “Off-White”. Which ultimately became the brand that propelled him to becoming one of the most prominent and beloved American fashion designers of our generation.





Entrepreneur, DJ, Architect, Creative
